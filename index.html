Beauty that feels like you *

Welcome to
Pourelle.

Pourelle - inspired by the French phrase "Pour Elle," meaning "For Her" - is a women-only beauty salon in AlSayh, Bahrain. It is a soft, private space for everyday beauty, celebrations, and the small rituals that help you feel put together.

From intricate henna artistry to hair styling, lash extensions, nail care, threading, and relaxation, the salon is built around comfort, care, and easy WhatsApp booking.
